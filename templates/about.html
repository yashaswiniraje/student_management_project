About Us

MET BKC is a leading institution dedicated to providing high-quality education and fostering academic excellence. With a rich history spanning several decades, we have consistently upheld our commitment to shaping bright minds and preparing them for a successful future.

Our campus is equipped with state-of-the-art facilities, experienced faculty members, and a vibrant learning environment. We offer a wide range of programs and courses, ensuring that each student finds the right path to their desired career.

Mission

  • To empower our students with knowledge, skills, and values that will enable them to contribute positively to society and achieve their goals.
  • Preparing Graduates capable enough to practice effectively and responsibly to technological changes and pursue advanced engineering.
  • Inculcate applied research to support and contribute to the social and economic well-being of citizens and organizations in nearby region.
  • Cultivate the entrepreneurial spirit and linkage of engineering education and business for technology commercialization.
  • Evolve thoughtfully to the changing needs of industry and surrounding world.

Vision

  • To become a global center of excellence in education, fostering innovation, research, and holistic development.
  • To develop Institute as a Centre of Excellence for developing globally accepted professionals having application orientation, lifelong learning approach towards socio-economic growth of stake holders and the Society as a whole.
